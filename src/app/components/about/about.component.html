History

Innovil wallet was founded on 7th September, 1906 by a group of eminent businessmen from Mumbai. The Wallet was under private ownership and control till July 1969 when it was nationalised along with 13 other services. Inovil wallet was originally promoted in 1994 by Innovil Limited, an Indian financial institution, and was its wholly-owned subsidiary.
